## Retrospective Framework

After completing a task, analyse the process:

### What Worked Well
- Which approaches were effective?
- What tools or techniques saved time?
- What decisions turned out to be correct?

### What Didn't Work
- Where did we get stuck or waste time?
- What assumptions were wrong?
- Were there any surprises or unexpected issues?

### What to Change
- What would we do differently next time?
- Are there patterns we should codify (new skills, tools, templates)?
- Were there missing tools or information that slowed us down?

### Action Items
- Concrete, specific improvements to make
- Each action should be assigned and trackable
- Focus on high-impact, low-effort improvements first
